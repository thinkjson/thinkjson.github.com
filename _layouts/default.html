About Mark Cahill

I am a web application developer working at a small private university, and finishing a second Bachelor's degree in computer science. I'm on the UI team for Saiku, an open source analysis tool, and an active member of the open source community.

I am passionate about scalable web services and elegant user interfaces, and feel that the web provides a unique opportunity to change the way we think about software and the way we interact with it.

The best place to find me is on freenode in ##saiku between 8am and 4:30pm EST M-F. You can also find me on Twitter, shoot me a message on Github, or use my Contact form.
